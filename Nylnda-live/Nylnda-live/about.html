About Us

Nylnda is a SNI based social app to connect people across the globe. It creates a hyper-local market and share your valuable
moments with your dear and near ones.

Nylnda started in 2017 as a small team of passionate designers, engineers, and entrepreneurs gathered around the idea that
SNI (Super Natural Intelligence) based social network.


Our research and developement has brought us to release the best SNI based social app in the world.
